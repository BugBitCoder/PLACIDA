# 🎨 Credits & Acknowledgments

Placida was built with care, drawing inspiration and tools from the following sources.

## 🌈 Design & Aesthetics
- **Design Inspiration**: The visual identity was inspired by the **MindBridge** concept on Dribbble, focusing on accessibility and calm user interfaces.
- **Color Palette (Custom Dark HSL)**:
  - **Base Background**: `#0d0f1a` (Deep Space)
  - **Primary Purple**: `#7c6af7` (Vibrant Lavender)
  - **Secondary Teal**: `#5ec4b6` (Muted Seafoam)
  - **Glassmorphism**: Custom opacity-based blur effects.

## 🛠️ Libraries & Tools
- **Visualization**: [Chart.js v4.4.0](https://www.chartjs.org/) (Loaded via CDN).
- **Typography**: [Google Fonts — Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter), chosen for its readability and modern feel.
- **Icons**: Standard **Unicode Emojis**, used to keep the project lightweight and dependency-free.

## 🧘 Wellness Resources
- **Breathing Technique**: The **4-7-8 Breathing** pattern is based on the work of **Dr. Andrew Weil**, a leader in integrative medicine.
